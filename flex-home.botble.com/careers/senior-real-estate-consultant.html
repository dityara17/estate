Senior Real Estate Consultant

Location: Ho Chi Minh, Viet Nam

Salary: From 1500$

Posted at: 2019-11-30

BENEFITS FOR YOU

- Basic salary 500$ / month.
- Access to diverse sources of goods and opportunities from reputable investors.
- Retrospective salary policy up to 2400$-7000$ / year.
- Commission of 22-50% and a commission 1000$ / successful transaction in the month.
- Effective bonus 0.6-3% of annual revenue.
- A place to build your personal brand in a standard and different way.
- Attend seminars on real estate every 6 months.
- Participate in the course on negotiation skills (Win - Win), language of sales (Language of Sales), customer mind marketing (SOI), customer conversion from social networks (Go Social)
- Unlimited promotion opportunities.
- Working with professional space and modern open office design.
- Welfare policy, domestic and foreign tourism ...
- Decentralized, democratic, financial transparency, considering brokerage is a business partner.
- Environmentally friendly, is the place where brokers are connected with regular activities

 

JOB DESCRIPTION

- Quantity: 30 employees
- Find and exploit potential customers for real estate products valued from 2000 USD / m2 in the high-end and luxury segment;
- Deploying activities to find customers, coordinate with teams to realize sales targets
- Advise customers on information related to products and projects of the Company;

 

JOB REQUIREMENTS

- Graduating from college or higher
- Good appearance, male height from 1.68m, female from 1.58m. Age from 22-35 years old
- Priority to candidates who know foreign languages ​​and have 1 year or more experience in the field of real estate, banking and finance.
- Have financial goals, work and life.
- Desire to achieve big goals.
- Diligent, persistent, honest.
